# 🎥 Demo video — full script (~85 seconds)

## Recording setup

1. Open **http://localhost:8501** in Chrome (full window, no other tabs visible)
2. Make sure the sidebar is on **USDC Depeg — SVB Repeat** (the default)
3. macOS: Cmd+Shift+5 → "Record selected portion" → drag over the browser window
4. Hit Record. Wait 1 second. Then start narrating.

---

## What to do AND what to say

Follow this beat-by-beat. Read the **Say** lines aloud at a normal pace. Do the **Show** actions in time with the words.

---

### 🎬 [0:00 – 0:10] — Hook

**Show:** Hero header at the top of the page (don't move yet).

**Say:**
> "In 2022, Terra collapsed and dragged down Anchor, Three Arrows Capital, Celsius and Voyager in ten days. The dependencies were visible on-chain — nobody had a tool to see them. *This* is that tool."

---

### 🎬 [0:10 – 0:25] — What it is

**Show:** Slowly scroll down to the contagion graph. Hover over the **Aave V3** node so the tooltip appears (TVL, exposures, blurb).

**Say:**
> "DeFi Domino maps the top twenty-five DeFi protocols and the thirty-nine real exposures that connect them — every PSM reserve, every collateral concentration, every restaking link, sourced from Aave, Maker, Lido and EigenLayer governance docs."

## After recording

1. Trim any silence at start/end (QuickTime Player → Edit → Trim)
2. Upload to YouTube → set visibility to **Unlisted** → copy share link
3. Paste into "Link to Demo Video" on the form

## Pacing tips

- 85 seconds is the target. If you go over 100, re-record — judges drop off after 90.
- Don't pause to think mid-sentence. Read the script. Re-record if you stumble.
- Move the mouse smoothly — don't do nervous jitters near the cursor.
- The graph takes ~1.5s to physics-stabilize on first load; let it settle before the camera lingers on it.
